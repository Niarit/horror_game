No one ever heard of something like this before.
The sheer strangeness of it makes everyone thinking about it dizzy.
As a detective though you heard and saw many strange things, but this made you confused as well.
Someone stole the Christmas! The gifts, the trees, the festive mood... even the days from the calendar are gone.
Your job is to find out who did this.
Your suspects and clues lead you there, but will you be able to find the answer?
